About kolkata


Kolkata is a marvelous place famous for its ,food, tradition. the whole city is full of attraction.There are a lot of visiting spots. Few visiting spots are- Botanical Garden, Indian Museum, Zoo, Howrah Bridge, Banks Of Ganga, and lots more places. Kolkata has a big hub of markets and shops. We can get good food at a very less price. The tram transport system of Kolkata is also very famous. Kolkata during its Durga puja just looks like a city full of happiness, colours and celebrations. At time of puja the city is lit with different types of lightings. The best time to visit Kolkata is the time of Durga puja. During that time thousands of people gather to visit the pandals. The event of Durga puja continues for about 5days. Also during that time different wildlife species are found at the outskirts of the city. The temperature is a little cold and hazy during that time. Many tourists come to visit Kolkata that time. Kolkata is also a great place to explore Historical Buildings and architectures. People can see many old British times buildings while walking on its old roads. Coming to Kolkata during the time of Christmas is also a good time. Park street in Kolkata looks like queen at the time of christmas. The weather is cold at that time. Going to Kolkata during the season of summers is not a good idea as the weather conditions are humid and extremely warm.
